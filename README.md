**Kreator Frame Dashboard** is a Flutter-based open-source template designed for creators to showcase and share their widget and wallpaper packs compatible with Kustom Apps (**KWGT** & **KLWP**). It provides a professional bridge between your creative work and the Google Play Store.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This project is currently in its **Beta phase**. While fully functional for distribution, contributions and bug reports are welcome to enhance its stability and features.

---

## üöÄ Key Features

*   **Kustom Integration**: Native support for `.kwgt` and `.klwp` files.
*   **Wallpaper Dashboard**: Dedicated section to highlight your high-quality wallpapers.
*   **Fully Customizable**: Easily change app names, icons, package IDs, and splash screens.
*   **Production Ready**: Built with deployment to the Play Store in mind.
